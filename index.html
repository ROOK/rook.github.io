Cloud-Native Software Defined Storage that is open, flexible, scalable and integrates with your environment

Simple storage management and effortless deployments. Let Rook self-heal and self-balance your cluster.

Rook has an active developer and user community. It’s open source software released under the Apache license.

Whether you’re scaling up or down, Rook is flexible enough to meet your businesses ever changing storage needs.

One platform for all of your application data needs.

Rook natively supports enterprise-class storage services including file system, block storage, and object storage. Speed up the time you need to adapt to changing business needs, simplify your infrastructure and fully utilize your storage resources.

Software-defined storage for your cloud-native apps

Built as a software defined storage platform, Rook runs on commodity hardware, providing cost saving and flexibility for you. Docker, Kubernetes, or OpenStack developers can take advantage of our support for diverse protocols such as NFS, iSCSI or S3.

Based on enterprise grade Open Source Ceph

Rook builds on the Ceph open source project, which is used by many of Fortune 100 companies and backed by thousands of enthusiastic contributors. We did the heavy lifting to take advantage of distributed storage that Ceph provides, so you don't have to.

Easy to deploy and maintain

Maintaining open-source based storage is difficult and consumes a lot of development resources. Rook helps you start up a cluster with only a few lines of code and is done in minutes, not days or weeks. Rook self-heals and automatically manages your clusters.

Scale up or down anytime

Rooks self-management features allow you to add or remove nodes easily and takes care of configuration and balancing for you. This gives you the flexibility to scale your clusters up or down anytime your storage needs change.
